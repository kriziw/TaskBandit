Privacy Policy

TaskBandit is designed to work with a user-configured backend server.

The application itself does not collect or store personal data independently. Any data entered into the app is transmitted to and stored on a server specified and configured by the user during setup. This server may be self-hosted, operated by the developer, or provided by a third party.

TaskBandit does not control or take responsibility for how data is stored, processed, or secured on external servers configured by the user.

No data is collected by the developer outside of the infrastructure explicitly configured by the user.

Users are responsible for ensuring that their chosen server environment complies with applicable data protection requirements.
